UAV Procurement Landing Page

The procurement system is where the Bill of Materials get entered into so that we can drive purchase orders and shipping request. Below is just a general overview of how to access the baseline information.

Accessing Procurement

Procurement is a Microsoft Access Database System. The icon to launch the system will be installed on your desktop by the IT department.

Start by Selecting the Procurement Icon

  

When the application opens you will see the Images to the right.

Select from the right side menu AV BOM.

From the AV BOM Sub menu select BOM Maintenance.

When the large Maintenance window Appears enter the job number in the appropriate text field and hit enter.
You can toggle the views By selecting either of the
Summary or Detail satellite Buttons.
