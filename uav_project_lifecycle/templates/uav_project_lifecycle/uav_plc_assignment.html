Kick Off Meeting

Every project that is done at USIS Audio Visual starts with a kick off meeting. Everyone listed above is included in the meeting with the addition of the  Project Management admins who also attend to take notes. This meeting is the transfer of knowledge from the Sales and Design team to the implementation team.

The standard is that we have a Kick Off within a week of project assignment. The meeting is initiated by either the Account Executive of record or the assigned Project Manager.
