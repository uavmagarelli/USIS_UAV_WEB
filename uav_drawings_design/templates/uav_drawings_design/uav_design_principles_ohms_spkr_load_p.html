Calculating Mis Matching Ohms Speakers

If we have mismatching speakers then we would use (Sp1×Sp2) / (Sp1+Sp2) = total/(Sp1+Sp2) = Total Ω Impedance

In this example we have speaker 1 being an 8 Ohm Speaker and speaker 2 being 16 Ohms.

This would give us the following (8×16) / (8+16) = 128/24 = 5.333 Ω

Calculating Matching Ohms Speakers

If we have a pair of matching speakers then we would use (ohms rating of a speaker) / Qty of Speakers = Total Ω Impedance

In this example we have speaker 1 being an 16 Ohm Speaker and speaker 2 being 16 Ohms.

This would give us the following 16 / 2 = 8 Ω
