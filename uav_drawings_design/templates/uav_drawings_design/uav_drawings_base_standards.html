Drawings are sketched and grouped in designated AV series. The AV series are typically determined by the facilities set and that is normally coordinated loosely with the Sales Bill of Materials and the subsequent room type break down. The following is shows how drawings are typically numbered:

 AV-XX.YYY

AV = Audio Visual Department Drawing

TC = Data / Telecom Department Drawing

SC = Security Department Drawing

XX = System Type Number

YYY = Drawing Type

 

 

  

Audio Visual Specific Drawing Legend

The following is a break down of what the different numbers represent.
 

XX = System Type Number

00=GENERAL NOTES

01-99=SYSTEM NO (TYPICALLY PER ROOM)

 

 

YYY = Room Type Series Number

001-099=OVERVIEW & NOTES

101-199=AV ELECTRICAL PLANS & CEILING PLANS

201-299=AV AUXILIARY PLANS

301-399=WIRING DIAGRAMS

401-499=CONTROL DIAGRAMS

501-599=DETAIL DIAGRAMS

601-699=ELEVATIONS
