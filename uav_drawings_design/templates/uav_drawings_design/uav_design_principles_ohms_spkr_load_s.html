Calculating Ohms Speakers In Series

When calculating impedance of ohm speakers wired in series we add the ohms of each speaker in the series.

In this example we have speaker 1 being an 8 Ohm Speaker and speaker 2 being 16 Ohms.

This would give us the following 8 + 16 = 24 Ω
