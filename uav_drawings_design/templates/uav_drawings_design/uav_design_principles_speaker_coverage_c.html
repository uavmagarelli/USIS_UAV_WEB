Calculating The Diameter Coverage of a Speaker

Diameter at ear height = 2 * (Ceiling height - height of seated ear level) * tan(Speakers Nominal Coverage Angle / 2)

In this example we have a speaker wth a Speakers Nominal Coverage Angle of 120 Degrees going in a room where the ceiling is 12' tall.

This would give us the following 2 * (144 - 48) * tan(120 / 2) = 61.45 Inches
