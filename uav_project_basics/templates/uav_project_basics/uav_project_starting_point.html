UAV Projects Starting Point Landing Page

Every Audio Visual project starts with the same basic set of documents and completed work. Below represents what is to be expected at the start of each project prior to the explanations of these documents further in your reading.

Starting Documents

BOM = Bill of Materials

This document is the co-starting point of your engineering research and process. This document specifies the make, model and special pricing for everything sold on the project. It will also include the hours and associated costs sold on the project. The raw BOM from the sales folder should never be saved over or modified. Do a save as and work on your own private version of the document.

 SOW = Scope of Work

This document is the other co-starting point of your engineering research and process. This document specifies the make, model and special functionality requirements sold to the client for that particular system. The co-development of the sales SOW is what will drive the creation of the Theory of Operations document described later.

 100% IFC Set =  ISSUED FOR CONSTRUCTION

This document is the main point of your schematic creation and the field teams map to the site. Any project below the 100% IFC set means that the facilities for the project are still not completely confirmed. That will almost always have an affect on your BOM and could also affect the SOW as well.

This particular situation could arise if you are working on Enterprise clients. For these client we typical do both design and builds and often these clients see repetitively assigned Project Engineers. For those instances you may be brought in earlier than the approved Purchase Order as a design consultant. In those cases you should expect to assist in the creation of the 100% IFC—in a review and assist role and not a design and document creation role.
